My life in Italy

My name is Antonella, I'm a web designer since 10 years. I started to work in a web agency when I was still in high shool.

I went to the fine art academy, and afterwards I started to work as freelancer in Italy. I was teacher for two years in a privat school and I teached a Web course of 90 hours.

I worked with communication agencies as graphic desinger, in web agency as art director and web designer and also i did some project as toy designer.
