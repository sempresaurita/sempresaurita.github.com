About me

Italy

I am Antonella from Verona. During my high school years I started working for a web agency. Later I graduated from a fine art academy, and afterwards continued my carrier as a freelancer.

Taught 90-hour courses at a private school, worked with communication agencies as graphic designer, art director and web designer. I also had some projects as a toy designer.

Berlin

In love with Berlin, I decided to give up my Italian life and move here in 2011.

A fresh and stimulating city, perfect for designers and developers. Worked at DeliveryHero (Lieferheld) for almost 2 years, starting as a Web Designer and Front End Developer, later as the Design Team Leader.

After Lieferheld, I worked for kaufDA as Head of Interface Design for 2 years.

From August 2014 I’m a full-time freelancer again.
